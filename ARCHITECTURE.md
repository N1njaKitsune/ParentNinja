# ParentNinja — Architecture

## Overview
The parent section gives parents visibility into their child's progress,
receives notifications from instructor grading events, and manages family profile settings.

## Sections
- **Parent Dashboard** — Overview of child's activity and progress
- **Progress View** — Belt level, Aura stage, recent sessions
- **Notifications** — Grading updates, milestone alerts from instructor
- **Family Profile** — Account and family management
